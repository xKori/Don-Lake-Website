Biography

Attorney Don Lake Jr.

Schooling

Hoover High School, Glendale, CA -1964

University of Southern California - 1968

Loyola Law School - 1971

Admitted to the California State Bar in 1972

Legal Experience

1971 to 1978 - Private practice handling business litigation and personal injury

1978 - Joined a major law firm as a trial attorney representing the insurance companies as a defense attorney. The practice at this 125 person firm includes malpractice, personal injury and business litigation. I was a senior trial attorney handling numerous civil trials.

1983 - I began my own practice handling personal injury and business litigation cases.

I am a second-generation attorney

My father practiced law in downtown Los Angeles for 50 years

My cousin was a Superior Court Judge in Los Angeles

My other cousin was in private practice as an attorney. He formerly was a Deputy Attorney General with the State of California.

My brother is a partner in an insurance defense firm in Los Angeles
