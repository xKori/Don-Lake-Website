Frequently Asked Questions

Why do I need an attorney when I have been in an accident?

No one, not even an attorney, should represent themselves in an insurance claim or legal matter. Our office deals with insurance companies daily, we have handled thousands of cases just like yours, we know how to set up the claims, apply for all types of coverage's, take care of complex legal issues and make sure that the client receives the maximum dollar amount for their injuries and damages.

What do I do at the accident scene?

You should call the police if necessary, get the names of all parties and witnesses, get all the insurance information, take photos, and attend to all injured persons. Never admit fault at the scene of the accident. Always cooperate with the law enforcement. Take down all of the information on the parties, their insurance and the cars involved. Immediately call our office so we can help you.

Can you come to my home or office after the accident?

One of my managers will immediately come to your home or office to get you signed up and begin handling your case. We will refer you to a doctor if you don't have one to take care of your injuries.

Is your office fluent in Spanish?

Yes, my office staff is fluent in Spanish. We have successfully handled hundreds of Spanish speaking clients.

What are the charges for your services?

We never charge our clients any money upfront. We handle personal injury cases on a contingency basis. That is, we only get paid a fee of one third of the total settlement when we win your case for you. If there is no recovery, you aren't required to pay my firm any money. We do charge at the end of the case some small amount for the costs involved but again only if we win your case for you.

Why should I choose your firm instead of another law firm?

I have been handling personal injury auto accident cases in Southern California for over 45 years. I have tried numerous Superior Court jury trials. For many years I represented the insurance companies, so I know what they are going to do in advance. We are experts in the area of coverage issues in regard to auto cases. My staff has many years of experience in assisting clients on such cases. I personally negotiate each settlement to its conclusion for the client. No other attorney will be more vigorous in negotiating a client's settlement. I have the highest rating for quality of work and legal ethics as rated by Martindale Legal Publications.

Do you handle cases outside the Los Angeles area?

Yes, we have represented clients from Sacramento to San Diego and from Catalina Island to Palm Springs.

My office is open from 9:00 AM to 5:00 PM on normal business days, If you call after-hours, you can leave a voice mail or call my cell phone, which is noted on the voice mail and on my "Contact" page.
